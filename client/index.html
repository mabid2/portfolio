Why I became a Developer?

     Ever since I was a child, I have been intrigued by the way technology works. The dynamic movement in the tech industry convinced me to finally change my major from Industrial Engineering to Computer Programming because this skill set would allow me to develop my curiousity, and enhance my creativity in the field of technology.

     To quench my curiousity, I started an immersive program called Coding Dojo, where I learned how to be a sufficient web-developer. Now I am passionate about learning new technologies and working with people to create amazing projects that impact our community at large. I have the ability to easily pick up other technologies when needed and am committed to bringing positivity and confidence to the workplace everyday.
